Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common support questions

What information should I include in my support request?

+

Please include: detailed description of the issue, error messages, environment details (OS, versions), steps to reproduce, and any recent changes made to the system. Screenshots or logs are also very helpful.

Do you provide remote access support?

+

Yes, I can provide remote support through secure screen sharing or SSH access to your systems, with proper security protocols in place. All remote access is logged and requires your explicit permission.

What are your payment terms?

+

Payment is due within 30 days of service completion. We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments. For ongoing support contracts, monthly invoicing is available.

Do you offer ongoing support contracts?

+

Yes, I offer monthly retainer agreements for ongoing support with discounted rates and priority response times. Contact me to discuss custom packages based on your needs.

What time zones do you support?

+

I'm based in GMT+7 (Ho Chi Minh City time) but provide emergency support 24/7. Standard and priority support follow business hours, with some flexibility for urgent issues.

Do you provide training and knowledge transfer?

+

Absolutely! I provide comprehensive documentation, training sessions, and knowledge transfer to ensure your team can maintain the solutions we implement together.
