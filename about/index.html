About 1DevOps

Professional DevOps consulting and solutions for modern development teams, backed by 12+ years of hands-on experience in system administration, cloud infrastructure, and automation.

I'm Le Phuong Hieu, a Senior DevOps/SRE Engineer dedicated to simplifying complex systems and helping organizations build robust, scalable infrastructure that supports their growth and success.

My Story

Since starting my journey in IT in 2009, I've been passionate about making technology work better for people and businesses. What began as curiosity about Linux systems has evolved into over 12 years of professional experience helping organizations transform their development and operations processes.

My approach is simple: I simplify complex things. Whether it's designing a Kubernetes cluster from scratch, implementing CI/CD pipelines, or optimizing cloud infrastructure, I focus on creating solutions that are both powerful and maintainable.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work with diverse technologies and face varied challenges - from managing e-Library systems at Can Tho University to enabling P2P streaming infrastructure for internet cafes. Each experience has contributed to my comprehensive understanding of system administration, networking, and DevOps practices.
